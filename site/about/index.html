About Us

"What are you working on? How can I help?" The common refrain through our careers has been collaboration. We started Scampersand because complementary skills with a shared philosophy and enjoyment of our work are an amazing combination that produces extraordinary results.

What We Do

We have a few guiding principles as we approach a project:

  1. We invest in the up-front planning so that we can execute smoothly with a common understanding of the next steps and end product. Planning and execution are separate modes--if something comes up that calls for a change, we halt execution and toggle as a team to reassess the plan. We firmly believe that a disciplined process is the best way to avoid waste and frustration, and yields a better result.

  2. We investigate alternatives even when the answer seems obvious. As humans, our tendency is to adopt the first solution we think of. By forcing ourselves to consider alternatives, we often discover find solutions that are better and cost less!

  3. We have a third guiding principle, I'm just sure of it. Probably something to do with proactive communication and engagement with clients.
