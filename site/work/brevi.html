--- title: Brevi layout: project ---

Brevi helps small businesses advertise on Facebook easily by harnessing existing partner data to generate ads. As technical co-founder, Aron developed the system including six partner OAuth and API integrations, payment through Stripe, and a custom Facebook Graph client for making batch requests at scale. Amy contracted to the startup to provide UX and visual design for the Brevi product, as well as development of the e-commerce ad generation code.
