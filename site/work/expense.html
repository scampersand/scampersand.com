Problem

As a young couple fresh out of college, we were anxious to start on the right foot financially. We wanted to track our spending, but it was annoying to keep receipts and need to enter them into the computer at home.

Solution

Since mint.com wouldn’t exist for another decade, so we built a web-based expense tracker so we could enter our own expenditures from our desk computers at work. It was our first collaboration on a web project—Perl CGI with a PostgreSQL database—and we’re still quite proud of it.
