PagePart was a startup helping small businesses make mobile-friendly websites. The startup contracted Aron to build the system, which would import structured data from Facebook to generate an initial site then allow complete customization.

The first requirement was to support pre-made "parts" that could be plugged into a site, including Google Maps, Google Calendar, Twitter, OpenTable, Yelp, RSS feed, and others. The second requirement was to provide the site editor as a white-label product so agencies could offer it to their clients under their own brand. These goals were achieved and PagePart gained a number of fiercely loyal users during its run.
