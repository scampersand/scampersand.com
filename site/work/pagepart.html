--- title: PagePart layout: work ---

PagePart was a startup helping small businesses make mobile-friendly websites. The startup contracted Aron to build the system, which would import structured data from Facebook to generate an initial site then allow complete customization. There were two distinct challenges: first, to support a number of pre-made “parts” that could be plugged into a site, including Google Maps, Google Calendar, Twitter, OpenTable, Yelp, RSS feed, and others; second, to provide the site editor as a white-label product so agencies could offer it to their clients under their own brand. These goals were achieved and PagePart gained a number of fiercely loyal users during its run.
